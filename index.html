FAQ
Common questions.
Does this work on iPhone and Android? +
Yes. It's a single HTML file that opens in any mobile browser. On iPhone, open it in Safari and tap "Add to Home Screen." On Android, open in Chrome and tap "Add to Home Screen." It works exactly like an app on both.
Do I need to create an account? +
Nope. No account, no sign-in, no email required. Your stats save directly to your phone. Everything stays on your device.
How does Platinum work? +
You download the free app, then upgrade to Platinum inside the app for $5.99/month. You get a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime — no commitment.
Can a coach use this for a whole team? +
Yes — that's exactly what Platinum's Team Tracker is built for. Add your full roster, track each player's stats game-by-game, see team leaderboards, and use Team Chat to message the group. One coach with Platinum can manage the whole roster.
What stats does it track? +
Batting: AB, H, 1B, 2B, 3B, HR, RBI, R, BB, K, HBP, SB, CS, SAC, E — plus auto-calculated AVG, OBP, SLG, and OPS. Pitching: IP, ER, H, BB, SO — plus auto-calculated ERA and WHIP. All 17 stats are also explained in the Learn section.
Will my data transfer if I get a new phone? +
Stats are saved to your device's local storage. If you switch phones, you'd start fresh — but you can always look up your old game-by-game history before switching and note your season totals.
